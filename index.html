About Me

I'm a creative freelancer living in brooklyn that specializes in Video Production, having obtained a Bachelor's degree in Media, Culture, and the Arts from the King's College in 2014. I've happily lent my skills and support to a variety of production crews for visual mediums, assisting with photoshoots, film sets, interviews, live events, and more.

My skills grow a little more with each project I have the blessing to help with, but, at the end of the day, I'm just a soul whose intentions are good. Oh Lord, please don't let me be misunderstood.
